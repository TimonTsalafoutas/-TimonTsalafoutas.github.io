8th Generation Video Game Consoles

Playstation 4

The most succesful console of the generation clearly dominates its competitors . It had more than double sales that microsoft's second xbox one .

Xbox One

Microsft's console was good but it didn't have such big success like playstation 4 had. It met the expectations and was the second console in the market.

Wii U

Nintendo's Wii U was quite the failure as it didn't reach its sales goal. It had little to offer and passed unnoticed

Nintendo Switch

Nintendo's Switch made up for Wii U's failure with the nintendo Switch . Even though it is behind Xbox one in sales it has a unique feature that makes it portable. It also has very good exclusives and some games that were only on PC and no other console like Cuphead. Later Nintendo released Nintendo Switch lite which is basically the same but comes at a cheaper price without a console dock, making it exclusively portable.
